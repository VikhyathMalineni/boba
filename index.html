What is Boba?

Boba, also known as bubble tea, is a sweet and refreshing Taiwanese drink that combines tea, milk, and chewy tapioca pearls.

Types of Boba

  • Classic Milk Tea
  • Fruit Tea
  • Taro Milk Tea
  • Matcha Milk Tea
  • Brown Sugar Boba

Make Your Own Boba

Here's a simple recipe to create your own boba at home:

  • 1 cup of tapioca pearls
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 cups of tea (black or green)
  • 1/2 cup of milk (optional)
  • Sweetener of choice (sugar, honey, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water and cook tapioca pearls until they float (about 10 minutes).
  2. Drain and soak the pearls in a sugar syrup.
  3. Brew your tea and mix it with milk and sweetener to taste.
  4. Combine the tea and boba, and enjoy!
